About Utah Fencing Foundation (UFF)

As one of two Paralympic combat sports, wheelchair fencing is a blend of mental agility, speed, and tactical finesse. In 1960, the world would observe the first Paralympic competition at the Summer Olympics in Rome. Since then, adaptive sports have grown in accessibility and visibility and are now practiced in 34 countries.

 

In the spirit of bringing the sport of fencing to all, Utah Fencing Foundation (UFF) was established in 2014 as a 501c3 educational organization by Konstantin Gritsaev. A few years later, UFF partnered with Utah Swords Academy Fencing Club to develop a program specifically for parafencers.

 

 

Today, UFF is part of the Utah and southern Idaho U.S. Fencing division, and provides professional coaching, equipment, and parafencing demonstrations. Because of the important work that UFF does, students with locomotor disabilities are learning and experiencing the wonderful sport of fencing.

 

Donating to Utah Fencing Foundation

 

UFF actively seeks out under-resourced communities, schools, and organizations to partner with. If you would like to become a sponsor, host a demonstration, or find out more about how to start your parafencing journey, please contact us through our facebook page, email, or give us a call at 801-278-3568. All donations are tax-deductible and are accepted through PayPal, or Venmo and go towards equipment purchase, fees, and facility development.
